What We Do

The Flying Food Truck, pioneered by Joy Phadte, opened in the buzzing city of Mumbai in March 2019. Our aim is provide all our guests with an unforgettable experience as we take them on a multi-cuisine journey.We want to reach out to all our guests so you can create memories by having a Joyful time with us.

Come. Have a bite. Laugh a lot. Savor your journey as your palate flies through the world with us.
